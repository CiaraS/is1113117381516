Amazon Web Services (AWS): Infrastructure as a service

AWS is a cloud computing platform. It offers (Saas) and (Paas) but here I will outline it's offerings in regards to infrastructure as a service.

AWS was originally built for Amazon to use internally to handle it's online retail operations. It's huge number of data centers in many different availabilty zones (AZ) provides customers with an extremely reliable infrastructure that is resistant to failures.

It is widely used and is the basis for many major companies such as Netflix.

Gmail: Communication as a service

Gmail is a free email service owned by Google. It offers 15 gigabytes of free storage currently, a sizeable differnce from the 1 gigabyte offered in it's early stages in 2004

It automatically scans emails for malware and spam making it a very customer-friendly option

It is a very large communication platform as it has over 1.2 billion users worldwide currently and also holds the title of being the first app on the android platform to reach a billion downloads.
