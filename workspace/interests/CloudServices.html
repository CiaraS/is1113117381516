SalesForce.comSoftware as a service

As of 2016, salesforce is one of the most highly valued American cloud computing company as it has a market capitalisation that is now above $61 billion. The company specializes in software as a service and was set up in 1999 by Marc Benioff, Parker Harris, Dave Moellenhoff, and Frank Dominguez.

They offer a wide variety of cloud based services including: App cloud, Community cloud, Work.com, Data.com, Desk.com and Do.com

Cloud 9: Platform as a service

Cloud 9 is a cloud-based service that allows your to do things such as develop, code, debug and run a project through your browser. It allows users to develop their projects with a wide variety of poplular coding languages such as Javascript, Phython, HTML and PHP.
